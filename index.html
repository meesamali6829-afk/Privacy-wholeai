Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Whole AI. We prioritize your privacy above all else. Our platform is built on a Zero-Knowledge Architecture, ensuring that your intellectual property remains exclusively yours.

1. Code & Content Data

We do NOT permanently store your source code, prompts, or generated content on our servers. When you use a feature such as the modernizer, bug hunter, or AI builder, your input is processed in volatile memory and discarded immediately after the AI response is generated.

2. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a securely handled password to provide login and personalization features. This information is stored locally on your device and is not sold or shared with third parties.

3. Gmail Integration (Email Agent)

If you choose to connect your Gmail account, Whole AI requests permission to send emails on your behalf using the gmail.send scope only. We do not read, store, or have access to your inbox, contacts, or any other Gmail data. Access tokens are used solely to send the emails you compose within the app and are not stored on our servers.

4. Local Storage

Chat history, scheduled emails, app preferences, and project data are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers, and will be removed if you uninstall the app or clear app data.

5. Encryption & Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is protected using industry-standard 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption.

6. Third-Party Services

Whole AI may use third-party services (such as Google Sign-In, Gmail API, and advertising networks for reward-based credits) which operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Whole AI is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channel.
